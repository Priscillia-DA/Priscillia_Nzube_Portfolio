Who I Am

About Me

I am a Geology graduate from Enugu State University of Science and Technology, now making an exciting transition into Data Analytics. My scientific background has given me a strong foundation in critical thinking, pattern recognition, and evidence-based reasoning — skills that translate naturally into the world of data.

I work with Excel, Power BI, SQL, Stata, and Kobotool Box to collect, clean, analyse, and visualise data. Whether it's understanding business performance, decoding customer behaviour, or uncovering safety trends, I am driven by the story that data tells and the decisions it enables.
